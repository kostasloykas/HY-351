Welcome

The National Vaccination Campaign for covid-19 has started. It is approved for its safety, quality and effectiveness by the competent international and national organizations, the World Health Organization, the EMA (European Medicines Agency) and the FDA (US Food and Drug Administration). Vaccination, free for all, starts for people being in the front line of health, for vulnerable groups, those in closed structures / care structures and for those who serve in important state services. The National Vaccination Committee may change the priority depending on the number of doses available. The vaccine is optional, but necessary to protect ourselves and our fellow human beings. And we do not forget: We continue wearing a mask and keeping our distance for as long as necessary.
